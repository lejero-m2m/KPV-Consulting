SCHEDULE 3 – [Sales and Marketing report - KPV]  
Sales & Marketing:
We had our first resident trial of a free consultation with Life’s Next Stage to gain an understanding of the possible roadblocks that might result in the applicant withdrawing and this has been a great success. The consultation uncovered that the applicant had a large lifestyle block with a 20ft and 40ft container on site that required de-cluttering. The house was due to go to market in mid-January and still required work to be completed prior to photos being taken and the applicants where heading overseas to see family in Australia for 3 weeks over the Christmas period. The applicant stated this had become very overwhelming and they were considering withdrawing. KP Papamoa agreed to pay for Life’s Next Stage to get the home ready for photos and market and de-clutter both the home and containers as and when needed. This service has saved an application withdrawal and reinforced that they have made the right decision to move the Karaka Pines Papamoa. 
Residents within the village are having regular catchup’s, including Friday Happy Hour. To help build connections between existing and incoming residents Trudi has been inviting applicants to attend Happy Hour. This has worked well and is proving successful in growing the sense of community at Papamoa. 
A KP Papamoa newsletter has been introduced. The newsletter provides the residents with updates on what is happening in and around the village from both an activity and development standpoint. 
Deborah, Village Operations Manager, is now based at Papamoa a minimum of half a day a week for a growing operations presence. This allows residents to come and speak with her personally with any concerns allowing Trudi to continue to focus on sales.  
Radio and digital advertising continue and monthly full-page adverts in The Papamoa Post commenced from December. The billboard on Papamoa Beach Road has been erected for 2 months. Trudi has had both residents and potential residents’ comment on it, demonstrating it is working. 
KP Papamoa have sponsored 2 upcoming tournaments one at Papamoa Bowling Club on 24th March and one at Mount Bowling Club on 27th March. Trudi attends all tournaments to give a presentation, answer questions and distribute marketing material. 
The first 2026 open day for Papamoa is scheduled for Thursday 26th February.
