APPLICATION STRENGTH | APPLICATION STRENGTH
GREEN | Very Strong Sale (Low Risk)
A sale that is highly likely to proceed to settlement with minimal risk.
Sale is unconditional, or
Purchaser has an unconditional sale on their existing home, and
Client demonstrates clear commitment and readiness to proceed
Interpretation:
These are effectively “bankable” sales with a high degree of certainty around settlement timing and outcome.
BLUE | Standard Sale (Moderate / Acceptable Risk)
A sale progressing as expected, with no immediate red flags.
Conditional sale (e.g. subject to sale of home) but
Where timing is appropriate they list their home, property appears appropriately priced and actively marketed, and
Client engagement remains positive and committed
Interpretation:
These represent the core of the pipeline. While conditional, they are considered normal and expected to convert with standard sales management.
AMBER | Sale of Concern (Elevated Risk)
A sale where emerging issues may impact the likelihood of settlement.
Limited buyer interest in the client’s property, or
Signs of hesitation or reduced confidence from the purchaser, or
Feedback indicating the client may be getting cold feet
Interpretation:
These sales require active management and intervention to prevent deterioration. Without improvement, they may move into the at-risk category.
RED | At Risk of Loss (High Risk)
A sale with a significant likelihood of falling over.
Client shows low commitment or disengagement, or
Vendor expectations are unrealistic (e.g. overpricing with unwillingness to adjust), or
Clear signals the client may withdraw or is not progressing
Interpretation:
These are unstable sales with a high probability of not proceeding. Immediate action is required, though recovery may be unlikely without a material change in circumstances.
