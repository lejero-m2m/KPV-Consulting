## VARIATIONS BY TYPOLOGY

The fixtures and fittings schedule is consistent across all four Stage 1 typologies (Berkeley, Juniper, Melrose, Monteray). The only confirmed variation is:

| Item | Variation |
|------|-----------|
| Brick mortar colour | Varies per unit exterior colour selection (e.g. Grey mortar, Antique White) |
| Shower size/layout | May vary between plan typologies (as noted on features list) |
| Carpet/laminate areas | May vary between plan typologies depending on floor plan layout |

Exterior and interior colour schemes are selected per unit from the KPV colour options palette (Options 1-6 for interiors; Options A-D+ for exteriors). Refer to individual unit selection sheets for specific colour allocations.

## NOT INCLUDED IN THIS SCHEDULE

The following items are managed separately and are not covered by this fixtures and fittings schedule:

- Landscaping (driveways, patios, fencing, planting)
- Site works and civil infrastructure
- Subdivision and resource consent costs
- Furniture, curtains, and window treatments
- Whiteware (washing machine, dryer, fridge)
- Security systems and alarms
- Fibre/internet connection
