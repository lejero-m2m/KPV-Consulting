# Karaka Pines Rototuna — Typologies by Stage

**Source:** `KPV-Knowledge-Base/hamilton/rototuna hamilton.zip` (consented/working drawings PDFs extracted per unit pair).  
**Compiled:** 2026-04-15

Floor areas are taken from the **DWELLING AREAS** schedule on each consented plan. Where the building contains multiple units (duplex or terrace), the schedule gives the **whole-building total**; the **Per-unit** column divides by the unit count.

Two area numbers are shown where available: **over frame** (gross floor area over the framing) and **over cladding** (outer envelope). Floor areas are for the ground floor only — all Rototuna dwellings in this archive are single-storey.

## Confidence and caveats

- **Area figures** are taken directly from the "DWELLING AREAS" block on each consented plan and are reliable.
- **Bedrooms** are counted from floor-plan labels (e.g. `BEDROOM 1.1, 1.2, 2.1, 2.2` on a duplex → 2 beds/unit; `BEDROOM 1, 2, 3` → 3 beds/unit).
- **Bathrooms/ensuites** are counted from plan labels. Where a powder room is present, it's noted separately.
- **Garage type**: flagged **Bold** where the plan text explicitly says "SINGLE GARAGE" or "DOUBLE GARAGE". Where the label wasn't found in the text layer, it's marked *"(to confirm)"* and a best guess is shown — verify against the actual floor plan sheet before using for sales or consent work.
- **Lucerne 89–90** and **Stanaway 5–9** rows are incomplete because the archive contains only scanned/administrative PDFs for those units — OCR or a fresh copy of the working drawings would be needed to confirm.
