# Karaka Pines Waihi Beach — Typologies by Stage

**Source:** `KPV-Knowledge-Base/waihi.zip` (consented/approved building plans per unit pair).  
**Compiled:** 2026-04-15

Floor areas are from the **"Area over Brick / Area over Framing"** schedule on the approved plans (Generation Homes drawings). Each area value is **per unit** (the duplex plans show the figure repeated for each side).

All units in this archive are single-storey. Waihi Stage 1a contains only two typologies: **Berkeley** (duplex) and **Eastwood** (standalone).

## Comparison against Rototuna (Hamilton)

The same typology names appear in both villages but the Waihi builds are a different generation and don't match their Rototuna namesakes:

| Typology | Rototuna (per unit) | Waihi (per unit) | Key difference |
|---|---|---|---|
| Berkeley  | 123.6–135.2 m² over framing, 2 bed, ensuite + bathroom, single garage | 124.2–142.84 m² over framing, 2 bed, bathroom + powder (no ensuite), single or double garage | No ensuite at Waihi; larger Berkeley 201–203 variant has a double garage |
| Eastwood  | 133.6–138.7 m² over framing, 3 bed, ensuite + bathroom + powder, single garage | 163.97 m² over framing, 3 bed, ensuite + bathroom + powder, double garage | Waihi Eastwoods are significantly larger and have a double garage |

## Confidence and caveats

- **Area figures**: taken from the "Area over Brick" / "Area over Framing" block on the consented floor plan page. Each figure appears twice on duplex plans (one per unit), confirming per-unit values.
- **Bedrooms**: counted from floor-plan labels (`BEDROOM 1/2/3`).
- **Bathrooms**: Waihi Berkeley plans contain `BATHROOM` and `POWDER` labels but no `ENSUITE`/`ENS` label anywhere — this is genuinely different from the Hamilton Berkeley. Waihi Eastwood plans contain `BATHROOM`, `ENSUITE`, and `POWDER`.
- **Garage type**: determined from the garage door dimensions shown on the floor plan (4,800 mm door = double, 2,800 mm door = single). All four files have clear, unambiguous dimensions.
