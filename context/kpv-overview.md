## Business Model

Karaka Pines differentiates on a **capital gains model** — residents retain any capital gain on their unit at sale. Deferred Management Fees (DMF/DFF) are marketed as lower than the industry average. Residents choose their own sale price and whether to accept offers. This is the core commercial proposition used in all marketing.
